# Bike Purchase Analysis using Excel

## Project Overview
This project analyzes customer demographic and lifestyle data to understand the
factors influencing bike purchasing behavior.  
Microsoft Excel is used to clean, analyze, and visualize data to extract
actionable business insights.

## Business Objective

The objective of this analysis is to:
Identify customer segments more likely to purchase bikes
Understand how income, age, commute distance, and occupation affect purchase decisions
Support targeted marketing and sales strategies

## Tools & Techniques Used

Microsoft Excel

Data Cleaning & Formatting

Pivot Tables

Excel Functions (IF, COUNTIFS, AVERAGEIFS)

Charts & Pivot Charts

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains customer-level information including:
Demographics: Age, Gender, Marital Status, Education
Financial & Lifestyle Data: Income, Cars Owned, Commute Distance
Geographic Data: Region
Purchase Outcome: Purchased Bike (Yes / No)

## Analysis Performed

Bike purchase analysis by Age Bracket
Income comparison between purchasers and non-purchasers
Purchase behavior by Gender and Marital Status
Impact of Commute Distance on bike purchases
Regional comparison of bike purchase trends

## Key Insights

Middle-aged customers showed a higher likelihood of purchasing bikes
Customers with moderate income levels were more likely to purchase bikes
Short to medium commute distances correlated with higher bike purchases
Certain regions demonstrated stronger bike purchase trends than others

## Visualizations

The project includes Excel-based visualizations such as:
Bar charts for purchase comparison
Pivot charts for demographic segmentation
Income vs Purchase trend analysis
